STONE COLLECTION

Having an assemblage of more than 200+ selected options for flooring, cladding and outdoor space, Shiv Marble and Granite is the first choice of homeowners, builders and architects. The company relentlessly brings forth impeccable quality stones, which are flawless and can turn any concrete structure into an exuberant, lavish home. In the next two years, we plan to take our product portfolio to 1500+ options. Our products are available in thickness from 3.6 mm to 100mm.

The company is known all around for its high-end products, accessible at a competitive price range. At Shiv Marble and Granite, the flooring & cladding material is available in the range of INR 40 to INR 5000 / Sq.Ft.
