Why SMG?

Building long-term customer relations is a primary focus for us here at Shiv Marble and Granite. We are solutions- driven, pride ourselves on our integrity, and deliver on our promises. We believe our commitment to this ethos allows Shiv Marble and Granite to be considered as a world-class manufacturer and supplier of specialized marble & granite products. The key factors for which we have received admiration from our clients all across the country include:

Timely delivery

Ultra-modern infrastructural facility

Hard working team of professionals

Qualitative range

Ability to handle bulk orders

Cost effectiveness of the entire range

Ethical business policies

Shiv Marble and Granite is an eminent Indian stone enterprise and a leading global brand. Having an experience of more than 30 years, the company today stands at the forefront of the natural and artificial stone industry.

Shiv Marble and Granite has set up itself as an esteemed technologically - progressive stone company which is continually emerging to redefine and magnify the most mesmerizing creation, Mother Earth has honoured us with.

Shiv Marble and Granite has mastered the skill of procuring supreme quality stone blocks from quarries around the world and processing with international standards to ensure that products of the finest quality reach its customers. With a diverse portfolio of selected 200+ products, Shiv Marble and Granite caters to the overgrowing demands of all customer groups by offering the best value for their money.

Apart from importing finest quality blocks from various countries, Shiv Marble and Granite also exports its produce of perfectly crafted and properly packaged, versatile slabs of marbles, granites, quartz and many other natural stones to around 28 countries, in a varying thickness of 10mm to 100mm.

In the words of Brian Tracy, "Excellence is not a destination; it is a continuous journey that never ends." Shiv Marble and Granite truly lives by this belief and is ever-evolving to serve the customers better and bestow them with an experience of a lifetime.
