The Company

Shiv Marble and Granite - The Stone Company is an eminent Indian stone enterprise and a leading global brand. Having an experience of more than 30 years, the company today stands at the forefront of the natural and artificial stone industry. Shiv Marble and Granite has set up itself as an esteemed technologically - progressive stone company which is continually emerging to redefine and magnify the most mesmerizing creation, Mother Earth has honoured us with.

PRODUCTION CAPACITY

Over the span of 30 years, Shiv Marble and Granite has committed to continually discover rare and unique stones from various locations around the world. A premier destination for selective natural stones, Shiv Marble and Granite runs with a monthly production capacity of 5,00,000 Sq Ft. In addition to this, the company also has an unmatched ability to source products 10 times its production capacity, in no time from our peers with the similar level of quality control. Shiv Marble and Granite takes immense pride in always upholding modernization, technological advancement and development in the natural stone industry.
